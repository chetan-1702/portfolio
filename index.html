MSc · 2024–25

A Complexity-Based Analysis of Deepfake Detection Mechanisms

MSc Cyber Security & Pen Testing — Middlesex University London

Compared a CNN (XceptionNet) against a CNN-LSTM hybrid for deepfake detection on FaceForensics++ and Celeb-DF-v2 — not just on accuracy, but on training time, memory footprint and FLOPs. The hybrid edged out on accuracy but at a steep resource cost, which is the trade-off the whole thesis is built around.

BSc · 2024

Analysis of VPN Encryption Techniques, Vulnerabilities and Behaviours in Cloud and On-Premise Environments

BSc (Hons) Cyber Security & Digital Forensics — Middlesex University Mauritius

A meta-analysis of VPN tunneling protocols — GRE, IPSec and L2TP — comparing how their encryption and authentication mechanisms hold up against attacks like DoS, MiTM and data spoofing. Also weighs traditional on-premise VPNs against cloud-based deployments, and where the security/performance trade-off shifts between them.

GRE IPSec L2TP Encryption Authentication Cloud vs On-Prem

Attacks analysed

Key finding
  • Cloud-based VPNs cut cost and improve scalability, but introduce risks on-prem VPNs don't have
